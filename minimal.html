Chrome iOS fixed-layer stale bug

On iPhone Chrome iOS (not Safari iOS), scroll this page after first load. The fixed bottom tabbar drifts out of alignment with the visible viewport bottom, and only the cyan (100svh) marker is visible — the red (100dvh) and purple (100lvh) markers are clipped out by the fixed-layer paint boundary.

Tap the URL bar and cancel to force a compositor invalidation — the layout snaps back into alignment instantly.
